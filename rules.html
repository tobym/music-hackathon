    • About the Game

    There are four players, and one special CPU player, Hal9000 of Echonest.


    Each player is trying to accumulate as many points as possible in five(5) rounds of gameplay


    Each round begins with a question. E.g. "What is the best song to study to?"


    Each participating player provides an answer to the question.


    All answers are voted on by the participants. Players cannot vote on their own answer.


    Each vote on a song counts as 1 point.


    At the end of each round, the winner's song is played.


    A player's points are carried into the next round and will be totaled in their final score.


    The winner is player who has the most points at the end of five(5) rounds.
